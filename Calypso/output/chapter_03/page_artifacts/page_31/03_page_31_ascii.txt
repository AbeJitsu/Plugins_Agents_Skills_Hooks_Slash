│  [Body Text - 11pt Regular with italic emphasis]                           │
│  The fee simple freehold estate is the highest form of ownership interest  │
│  one can acquire in real estate. It includes the complete bundle of rights,│
│  and the tenancy is unlimited, with certain exceptions indicated below.    │
│  The fee simple interest is also called the "fee interest," or simply, the │

│  restrictions and private interests.                                        │
