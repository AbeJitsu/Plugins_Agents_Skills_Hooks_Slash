│  Conventional life estate                                                   │
│                                                                             │
│  [Body Text - 11pt Regular]                                                │
│  A conventional life estate is created by grant from a fee simple property │
│  owner to the grantee, the life tenant. Following the termination of the   │
│  estate, rights pass to a remainderman or revert to the previous owner.    │
│                                                                             │
│  [Body Text - 11pt Regular]                                                │
│  During the life estate period, the owner enjoys all ownership rights,     │
│  provided he or she does not infringe on the rights of the remainder or    │
│  reversion interest holders, such as by damaging the property or           │
│  jeopardizing its value. Should such actions occur, holders of the future  │
│  interest may take legal action against the property owners.                │
│                                                                             │
│  [Body Text - 11pt Regular]                                                │
│  The two types of conventional life estate are the ordinary and the pur    │
│  autre vie life estate.                                                     │
